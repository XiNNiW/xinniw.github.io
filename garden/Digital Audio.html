Digital Audio

:cc0: Digital audio is sound in digital form (see: digital sampling). It may also refer to a number of signal processing techniques for recording, transmitting, storing, generating, and modifying that digital representation either in real time or offline.

  • BLIT (Band-Limited Impulse Train)

  • Bandlimited Oscillator

  • Bandlimiting

  • DSF (Discrete Summation Formula)

  • FIR filter

  • FM Synthesis

  • IIR Filter

  • Linear Predictive Coding (LPC)

  • Performance Requirements of Real-time DSP

  • PolyBLEP

  • White noise

  • Aliasing

  • Digital Filter

  • Digital Sampling

  • MinBlep (Minimum Band-Limited Step)

  • Non-Causal Filter

  • Numerically Controlled Oscillator

  • Sample Rate Reduction

  • Window Function

  • Synthesis Methods
