All About Love

In All About Love hooks argues that we collectively experience a crisis of lovelessness and that this is due in part to a misunderstanding about what love is. She offers new visions of love and synthesizes a new definition:

An Act of will — to extend one's self for the purpose of nurturing one's own or another's spiritual development

She defines spiritual as the union of mind and body — the wholeness of a person.

This definition has profound clarity. Love is a verb. A choice. It raises all kinds of interesting questions about what it means to act in a loving way.
